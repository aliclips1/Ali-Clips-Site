Policies

Our commitment to providing a premium experience is reflected in our policies.

Cancellation Policy

We understand that schedules can change. Please provide at least 24 hours' notice if you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment. Cancellations made with less than 24 hours' notice may be subject to a cancellation fee of 50% of the service cost.

Late Arrival Policy

If you are more than 15 minutes late for your appointment, we may need to reschedule your service to avoid disrupting other clients. Please call us if you anticipate being late.

No-Show Policy

Clients who do not show up for their scheduled appointment without prior notice will be charged 100% of the service cost.

Cleanliness & Parking Policy

No parking on driveway. Arrive with washed or clean hair. Arriving with oily or dirty hair may result in cancellation.
